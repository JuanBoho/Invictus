What we do

Invictus Performance provides multiple service options that allow clients to tailor programs to their specific needs. Often information is given but not applied consistently or correctly. We believe the greatest learning is gained during real world settings when performers are asked to deliver at a high level. Our team of Coaches are at their best when they are able to stay with a client and re-emphasis lessons learned, course correct at critical junctures and provide feedback to ingrain skills.

Team needs can vary through the course of a season. Invictus provides the luxury of flexibility in order to find an optimal fit. Examples include; consulting, EAP services, counseling, training and the ability to expand or contract services when necessary.

We offer a myriad of empirically based evaluations to assess the current status and future needs of any group or individual. We also utilize the most current methods to evaluate talent to ascertain best fit and optimal potential for performance.

Workshops are customized to seamlessly integrate into the framework of your organization and deliver specific material and lessons in an easy to understand and readily applicable manner. Our professionals are highly experienced in providing a wide range of topics in an engaging and informative way. We utilize information obtained from our evaluations to help structure workshops to target specific and greatest needs.

Group or team sessions can include coaches, athletes, or both.

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Trust
  • Team dynamics
  • Leadership
  • Team values
  • Team norms
  • Mental toughness
  • Growth mindset
  • Goal setting
  • Building team commitment
  • Understanding team roles
  • Communication and feedback among players and coaches
  • Coach and player behavioral styles and preferences
  • Responsibility and accountability

Workshops and Presentations are aimed to:

  • Stress Management
  • Confidence Building
  • Leadership development
  • Communication skills
  • Performance anxiety
  • Increased motivation
  • Team building
  • Overcoming mental blocks
  • Coping with injury
  • Dealing with frustration
  • Crisis response
  • Life after sport or career
  • Addressing Mental Wellness

We target to have your team function like a highly disciplined and productive unit.

Our goal is to bring the techniques and processes used by the highest performing individuals and organizations to your business and its employees.

Personalized sessions

For individuals or a team interested in maximizing their potential utilizing lessons learned from high achieving groups or individuals.

Invictus is partnered with Futures Recovery Healthcare to provide comprehensive, confidential services to all of our clients in need of help with addiction or mental health issues.

Futures has worked hard to curate specialized programming to meet the needs of professionals with specific needs when considering Healthcare options. To accommodate these individuals we ask that you review the offerings of the Athletes Program, Orenda, and Rise. These programs are designed to complement the Core program and others offered at Futures.

The content and execution within many of these programs were developed with the input and efforts of Dr. Anderson and members of the Invictus team who serves as the Executive Director of Orenda and creator of the Athletes program.

Sports Analytics

In addition to other standard reliable and valid assessments Invictus utilizes a system that is at the core of their behavioral analysis program:

Sports analytics is the brainchild of our VP of Behavioral Analysis Dafna Aaronson. Sports analytics focuses on new ways to create and slice data, data that is much like that found on the back of an athlete’s trading card. At Invictus we through the innovations of Ms. Aaronson focus on understanding the front of the card - who the person is.

By understanding who the person is we are able to create a comprehensive framework for if, why, and how a person can be successful in a given environment. We accomplish this by streamlining a proprietary machine learning platform with an organization’s internal models and technology. In short, we help maximize scouting reports and recruiting for Athletic Organizations and help Companies to understand their candidates and existing personnel.

We do this while eliminating subjective assessments, increasing the probability of success, and delivering qualitative metrics which can be integrated with existing performance statistics. To put it simply, we use the power of a powerful computer system developed by IBM capable of answering questions posed in natural language to help you understand the people you will and have invested in.
